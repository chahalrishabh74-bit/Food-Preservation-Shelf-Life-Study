# Food Preservation Shelf Life Study

## Research Internship

Technology Business Incubator (TBI-GEU)

### Project Title

Natural Preservation Methods — Shelf Life Study

### Objective

To investigate natural preservation techniques and packaging strategies that enhance food shelf life while maintaining safety, quality, and regulatory compliance.

### Research Focus

- Natural antimicrobials
- Antimicrobial peptides
- Traditional preservation methods
- Packaging technologies
- Shelf-life estimation
- Food safety and FSSAI considerations

### Research Question

How can natural preservatives and packaging technologies be combined to improve shelf life and food safety while reducing dependence on synthetic preservatives?
