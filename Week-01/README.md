## Key Research Question
How can natural preservatives and advanced packaging technologies improve the shelf life and safety of food products while reducing dependence on synthetic preservatives?

## Major Research Gaps Identified
- Limited industrial-scale adoption of natural preservatives
- Regulatory and compliance challenges
- Need for optimization of preservation techniques without affecting sensory quality
- Lack of integrated studies combining preservation methods and packaging technologies
